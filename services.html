Our Services

The Energy Audit

Following a detailed process, LEE Energy reps bring their audit skills to bear. They develop an accurate understanding of your current systems. This serves as the foundation for an effective retrofit, expansion or remodel.

Design Solutions

Look to LEE Energy to develop a custom solution that fits your business. Our energy efficiency solutions take into account existing equipment, current and desired light levels, operating hours, occupancy and budget. We also help you prioritize opportunities based on ROI and availability of utility incentives.

Led Lights and More

No longer a niche product, LED lighting stands alone as the top dog in energy saving lighting options. With hundreds of LED retrofits under our collective belts (including highly specialized projects), consider the LEE Energy team your energy-efficient LED expert. We offer everything from lighting design services for customized solutions to standard retrofits and LED re-lamping.

Installation Excellence

How do we insure a successful install? Simple. Our rigorous installation process ensures your project will be a success. We are there every step of the way, so problems are not. We assign a LEE Energy Project Coordinator to each job and choose only licensed electrical contractors and other skilled firms that have what it takes to complete your installation properly.
